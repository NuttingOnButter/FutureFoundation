Mentorship

Mentors

Responsibilities

  • Mandatory Saturday Meetings Every Week
  • Partcipating In The Activities and Events
  • Attending Mentor Training and Mentor Meetings
  • Providing Your Mentee With Professional Oppurtunities, Work Enviroments, and Networking
  • Communicating With the Future Foundation 15 Staff and Giving Us Weekly Reports On Your Mentee
  • Commiting To Your Mentee For The Next 4-Years Of The Program

Eligibility:

  • Have To Either Be A Junior In College Or Older
  • Have to Pass The:
    1. Pennsylvania State Police Criminal History Check
      2.
    2. PA Department of Human Services Child Abuse History Clearance
      3.

If You Have Already Taken The Clearances, You Can Email The Documents To Us At FutureFoundation@gmail.com

Benefits

You will become a major part of the student's life and will move on with them. Some mentors have become intergral parts of their student's life and particpated in major events with their mentees even after the 4-years. Some mentors have helped their mentee unpack at college dorms, been invited to a marriage, or other life events. You will have the possibility to expand your own network with the studetns and other mentors at the program.

Application Progress

If you or someone you know fits the requirements and would want to be a mentor, feel free to start the application and start building America's future today.
